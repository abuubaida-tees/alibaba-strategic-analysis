## Overview
This repository contains a comprehensive strategic and financial analysis of Alibaba Group Holding Limited. The project evaluates Alibaba's transition from a traditional e-commerce giant into an "AI + Cloud" digital enterprise. It assesses the firm's dynamic capabilities, financial health, and market positioning against competitors.

## Key Financial Insights (FY2021 - FY2025)
* **Revenue Growth:** Reached US$130.3B in FY2025, demonstrating a steady rise.
* **Net Income:** Reached US$14.2B, reflecting an AI-driven boost in profitability.
* **Operating Margin:** Improved to 13.9% in FY2025 due to AI-driven expense optimization.
* **Return on Equity (ROE):** Achieved 13.3%, indicating superior capital deployment compared to regional competitors.
* **Liquidity:** The current ratio strengthened to 1.85, highlighting high short-term solvency.

## Strategic Frameworks Applied
* **VRIO Analysis:** Identified Alibaba's multi-platform ecosystem and Big Data/AI capabilities as sources of Sustained Competitive Advantage.
* **Porter's Five Forces:** Evaluated industry rivalry and the necessity of restructuring around AI and cloud efficiency.
* **Bowman's Strategy Clock:** Mapped Alibaba's transition to Focused Differentiation to secure high-margin enterprise markets.
